Application Samples

Templates for every stage of your application journey. Fill in your details and copy-paste them wherever you need them.

Standard Application Cover Letter

Cover Letter

Use for direct applications through job boards or company career pages.

Subject: Application for {JOB_TITLE} at {COMPANY} Dear {HIRING_MANAGER}, I am writing to express my interest in the {JOB_TITLE} position at {COMPANY}. With {YEARS} years of experience in Marketing Operations, I have developed a strong track record of building scalable processes, optimising marketing technology stacks, and driving measurable revenue outcomes. In my most recent role as {CURRENT_ROLE} at {CURRENT_COMPANY}, I {KEY_ACHIEVEMENT_1}. I also led {KEY_ACHIEVEMENT_2}, which resulted in {QUANTIFIABLE_RESULT}. My technical proficiency includes {SKILL_1}, {SKILL_2}, and {SKILL_3}, which align closely with the requirements outlined in the job description. I am particularly drawn to {COMPANY} because {WHY_COMPANY}. I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my background in marketing operations can contribute to the team's success. Thank you for considering my application. Kind regards, {YOUR_NAME} {YOUR_PHONE} {YOUR_LINKEDIN_URL}

Career Change / Transition Cover Letter

Cover Letter

Best when pivoting from an adjacent field into Marketing or Revenue Operations.

Subject: Application for {JOB_TITLE} — Marketing Operations Specialist Dear {HIRING_MANAGER}, I am applying for the {JOB_TITLE} position at {COMPANY}. While my background is rooted in {PREVIOUS_FIELD}, my experience with data analysis, process optimisation, and cross-functional collaboration has prepared me well for a career in Marketing Operations. Throughout my {YEARS} years at {CURRENT_COMPANY}, I have consistently operated at the intersection of data and process: {RELEVANT_EXPERIENCE_1}. I have also built proficiency in {SKILL_1} and {SKILL_2}, tools that are central to modern marketing ops stacks. What excites me about {COMPANY} is {WHY_COMPANY}. I am eager to bring an analytical mindset and a bias for action to your operations team and grow into a subject-matter expert under your leadership. Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to the possibility of discussing how my transferable skills can add value. Warm regards, {YOUR_NAME} {YOUR_PHONE} {YOUR_LINKEDIN_URL}

Post-Application Follow-Up

Follow-Up Email

Send 5-7 days after applying if you haven’t heard back.

Subject: Following up on {JOB_TITLE} application Hi {HIRING_MANAGER}, I wanted to follow up on my application for the {JOB_TITLE} position submitted on {DATE_APPLIED}. I remain very interested in the opportunity to bring my experience in {SKILL_1} and {SKILL_2} to {COMPANY}. I recently {RECENT_ACHIEVEMENT_OR_UPDATE}, which I believe would directly support the team’s goals around {COMPANY_PRIORITY}. Please let me know if there’s anything else I can provide to support my application. I’d welcome the chance to discuss how I can contribute. Best regards, {YOUR_NAME}

Post-Interview Thank You

Follow-Up Email

Send within 24 hours of your interview.

Subject: Thank you — {JOB_TITLE} interview Hi {HIRING_MANAGER}, Thank you for taking the time to speak with me today about the {JOB_TITLE} role. I really enjoyed learning about {SPECIFIC_TOPIC_DISCUSSED} and how the team approaches {CHALLENGE_OR_PROJECT}. Our conversation reinforced my excitement about {COMPANY}’s work in {AREA}. In particular, what you shared about {SPECIFIC_INSIGHT} resonated with my experience in {RELEVANT_EXPERIENCE}. I look forward to hearing about next steps, and please don’t hesitate to reach out if I can provide any additional information. Warm regards, {YOUR_NAME}

My name is {YOUR_NAME}, and I’ve spent the last {YEARS} years in Marketing Operations, specialising in {SPECIALTY_1} and {SPECIALTY_2}. In my current role at {CURRENT_COMPANY}, I’ve {KEY_ACHIEVEMENT}. I’ve also used {TOOL_1} and {TOOL_2} extensively, which I noticed are part of your stack. I’d love to learn more about the team’s priorities for the next 6–12 months and share how I might contribute. If you have 10 minutes in the coming week, I’d be grateful for a brief chat. Thank you for considering my outreach. Best, {YOUR_NAME} {YOUR_LINKEDIN_URL}
