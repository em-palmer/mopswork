Scoring Methodology

Every job is scored from 0 (excluded) to 100 (perfect match). Here's how it breaks down.

1. Location Eligibility

Country must be United Kingdom. Roles outside the UK are excluded (score 0).

Option 1 — Remote: Remote UK roles qualify. Work type scores 20 points.

Option 2 — Hybrid: Hybrid UK roles within ~2 hours commute of Bridgwater. Includes London (train), Reading (car/train), Oxford, Southampton, Cardiff, Birmingham, etc. Work type scores 10 points.

Option 3 — Onsite: Only within the Bristol–Exeter M5 corridor (Bristol, Exeter, Bath, Swindon, Gloucester, Cheltenham, Taunton, etc.). Onsite outside the corridor is excluded. Work type scores 0 points.

2. City max 20

Bristol-Exeter corridor cities earn the most points.

Bristol 20 Exeter 20 Bath 20 Swindon 20 Gloucester 20 Cheltenham 20 London 10 Reading 10

3. Work Type max 20

Remote UK roles score highest.

Remote 20 Hybrid 10 On-site (corridor only) 0

3. Salary max 20

Higher salaries get more points. Roles under £60k score 0 for salary.

Less than £60k 0 £60k – £90k 10 £90k – £120k 20 £120k+ 20

4. Seniority max 20

Manager-level and above scores higher.

Manager 20 Head of 20 Lead 20 Director 20 Senior 20 Analyst 10 Specialist 10

5. Keyword Match max 20

Keywords in the job title (or description) determine category relevance.

High relevance (+20)
Marketing Operations 20 MarTech 20 Marketing Technology 20 GTM Engineer 20 Go-to-Market Engineer 20 Marketing Data 20 Data Analytics 20
Medium relevance (+10)
Sales Operations 10 RevOps 10 Revenue Operations 10 Marketing Automation 10 Lifecycle Automation 10

Auto-exclude (-50)

Titles containing these keywords are immediately rejected — score = 0:

Sales Manager Account Executive Business Development SDR BDR Account Manager Inside Sales Sales Rep Customer Success Manager Presales Solution Architect Product Manager / PMM National Accounts Field Marketing / Sales Lead Generation Talent Acquisition Chief of Staff Executive Assistant Supply Chain Manager Growth Marketing Manager Consultant Commercial Director Design Engineer

6. Skills Bonus up to +10

Extra points for desired skills mentioned in the description. Each matching skill adds 3 points, capped at 10.

HubSpot Salesforce Marketo Tableau Power BI SQL Google Analytics CRM API Python A/B Testing ABM Lead Scoring Lifecycle

Maximum score: 100

City (20) + Work Type (20) + Salary (20) + Seniority (20) + Keyword (20) + Skills (10 bonus)

Any job with an excluded keyword in the title is immediately scored 0.
